# Content Map

## 1. Content status labels

Every business-specific content item must be treated as one of:

- **Verified:** supported by a current official source.
- **Reference-supported:** visible in supplied material but still may need freshness verification.
- **Demo:** invented only to demonstrate the experience and labelled accordingly.
- **Unknown:** omit or present as unverified; never state as fact.

- Store prices as numeric PKR values for functionality.
- Use internally consistent demo values.
- Show one visible concept-price notice.
- Ask for final confirmation in WhatsApp message.
- Do not state demo prices as current Burger Buddies pricing.

## 12. Imagery policy

Use, in priority order:

1. Clean Burger Buddies product image or crop.
2. Focused crop from supplied product post when usable.
3. Clearly temporary high-quality food placeholder.

Do not use:

- full social-profile screenshots as product images
- posters containing large embedded prices/text
- images from the Emmanuel reference as Burger Buddies products
- fake ratings or restaurant logos from other brands

## 13. Prohibited copy

Do not include:

- Official Burger Buddies Website
- Trusted by thousands
- Number-one or best-in-city claims
- Guaranteed delivery times
- Verified ratings or review counts
- “Our customers” in a way implying Ibrahim owns the restaurant
- fabricated discounts
- fabricated branch information
- fabricated opening hours
