## Reference characteristics

- Three coordinated mobile screens: discovery, product details, and cart.
- Very dark background and layered charcoal panels.
- Bright amber/yellow primary actions.
- Large, appetizing food photography.
- Rounded search, filter, quantity, and checkout controls.
- Product details led by a large hero image and overlapping information panel.
- Compact cart rows and elevated order summary.
- Native-feeling mobile bottom navigation.
